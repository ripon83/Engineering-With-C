An Engineering student, Bhagyashree, believe on her luck. She always tried to 
become the luckiest person in the world. Therefore, she always prayed to the God 
to become lucky. 

One day, a C programming examination was scheduled, but Bhagyashree had studied
only ten questions. Surprisingly, the same ten questions appeared in the question 
paper. By the grace of God, she became the luckiest person that day. As a result, 
she wrote the exam very well and scored 100 out of 100.

After 3 years, all students have got jobs in the campus placement, except for 
Bhagyashree. So, we should not try to become a lucky person. 

The luckiest people often become the most unfortunate later in life, while 
those who seem the most unlucky often become the luckiest in the future. So,
believe on hardwork. Work is God.

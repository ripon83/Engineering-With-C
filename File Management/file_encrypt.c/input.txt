John: Oh God. I want to become the greatest boxer. Please help me achieve my goal.

Suddenly, God started hammering John. John could not tolerate God's hammering. 
John asked God: Why are you hammering me so hard?
God: You wanted to become the greatest boxer. So, I am helping you.
John: Is there another way to become the greatest Boxer?
God: No. Without torturing your body, you cannot win a single match.

John quits dreaming of becoming the greatest boxer.

Ganesh asks his father- "When will you allow me to marry?"
Father- "Tomorrow"
The next day, Ganesh to his Father-
Ganeshji: "Should I marry today?"
Father: "Tomorrow".
It continues, and "Tomorrow" never comes into Ganeshji's life.

Sometimes, the same happens in our lives, too. We feel that 
we will surely study from tomorrow and that tomorrow never 
exists in our lives. So, start studying today, right now. 
Tomorrow never exists.
